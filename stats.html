American Statistical Association and the P value

Posted on July 28, 2016

Recently, the American Statistical Association issued a statement over the misuse of P values. This was also picked up by Nature News. This whole affair is, of course, just the latest in the great reproducibility crisis in science, I recommend reading at least something on the issue. Noah Snyder-Mackler shortly discusses the recent ASA statement in the Molecular Ecologist. It's a nice short explanation of the whole mess for those of us in a real hurry.
